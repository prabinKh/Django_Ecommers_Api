Game Controller or Gamepad; Is It Same As Joystick?

People often mix up the concept of Gamepad and Joystick. Although both of the devices have the same function, there is a significant difference between them. Before deciding on what you should buy, let's know about the real difference between Game Controller and Joystick. A Gamepad is a form of game controller that is held in two hands where input is given by the fingers. A joystick, on the other hand, is an input device made out of a stick that pivots on a base and sends its angle or direction to the device it controls. In essence, both of them serve as input for video game consoles. Joysticks, on the other hand, can also be used to control devices like cranes, lorries, underwater unmanned vehicles, wheelchairs, security cameras, and other things.
