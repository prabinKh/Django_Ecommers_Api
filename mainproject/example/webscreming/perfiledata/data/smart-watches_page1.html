And as people become more and more health-conscious, there is a rise of popularity and demand for fitness trackers and Smartwatches in Nepal. Smartwatches are also equipped with lifestyle-enhancing features like pedometers that track the number of your steps throughout the day. With surge in demand of personal health condition tracker, new brands are increasing in Nepal. With the competion in Smartwarch price in Nepal is expected to go down benefiting local customers.

In addition to fitness tracking, Smartwatch also provides the ease of accessing your notifications straight from the watch itself. Notifications such as social media notifications, message notifications, missed call notifications, and message notifications can be easily accessed from the smartwatch once it gets connected to your smartphone.
